Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti is the simplest, yet most delicous dish you could prepare. it's super easy to make, yet tastes amazing, and even has a good amouht of calories and protein if you are trying to pack on some muscle.
    Ingredients
  • Raw spaghetti
  • Sauce of choice
  • Cheese of choice
    Recipe:
  1. Boil Water
  2. Place spaghetti
  3. When spaghetti are edible and not too chewy, strain out the water
  4. Mix with sauce of chocie which you should have warmed up on the side
  5. Use a cheese grater with cheese of choice over spaghetti
